About Us

SwitchCar is the India's premier peer-to-peer car rental market place. We let owners make money by renting out their cars or vans when they’re not using them, and help drivers save money through cheaper more convenient, local car hire. The SwitchCar team is dedicated to making better use of the cars on India's roads. The idea was proposed in February 2016, after the implementation of Odd Even Rule in January. SwitchCar plans to operates throughout India, and will have thousands of cars in convenient, local locations.

How It Works

For Drivers

  • >  A few simple checks, a friendly profile photo and you're all set for great value, local rentals.

  • > Search the site to find a nearby car at the right price. Book and pay securely online.

  • > Meet the owner to pick up the keys, or access them from a handy key safe with a code sent to your mobile.

  • > Drop the car back and make sure everything's in order. Share your feedback with other Club members.



For Owners

  • >  A simple online sign-up process. Then set your car's availability for times that suit you, at prices you choose.

  • > Receive rental requests from vetted members in your neighbourhood - you control who you rent to.

  • > Meet your renter face to face or use a secure Key Safe for convenient remote hiring.

  • > Make sure everything’s in order. If there’s anything amiss, we’re here to help.
